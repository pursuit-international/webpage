Spotlight Events

IN-PERSON TRAINING

All accepted Pursurers are required to attend 4 weekend spotlight events. Featuring Pursuit’s trained coaches, university partners, and other international professionals, these spotlight events will provide you with intensive, in-person training in the form of simulations, case studies, competitions, guest lectures, and panel discussions.

International Business Project

Mock International Company

After learning key concepts from our web-based curriculum, you will have chance to apply this knowledge in real life as you work on a business-related project with a team of students based overseas. There are strict requirements for this project and will focus on all parts of running/managing an international business including marketing, management, finances, operations, growth & strategy, and entrepreneurship.

Global Conferences

Company visits to a variety of industries

Upon completion of the training, all Pursurers are required to attend two 7-day global conferences located in Boulder, Colorado (USA) and in Beijing, China. The conferences will feature an in-depth examination of various business topics as well as company visits to a variety of industries including tech, service, hospitality, manufacturing, and retail.
